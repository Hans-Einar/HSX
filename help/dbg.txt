usage: dbg <command>

Commands:
  attach <pid>           attach debugger to PID and report register snapshot
  detach <pid>           detach debugger from PID and clear breakpoints
  regs <pid>             display registers for PID
  cont <pid> [cycles]    continue execution; optional cycle budget (default 1000)
  step <pid> [count]     execute count instructions (default 1)
  break <pid>            request an asynchronous break at next instruction
  bp list <pid>          list software breakpoints for PID
  bp add <pid> <addr>    add breakpoint at address (hex or decimal)
  bp remove <pid> <addr> remove breakpoint at address

Examples:
  dbg attach 1
  dbg bp add 1 0x120
  dbg cont 1
  dbg step 1 5
  dbg regs 1
