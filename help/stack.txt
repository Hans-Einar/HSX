stack <subcommand>

Subcommands:
  bt <pid> [--frames N]   Show backtrace (default 32 frames) and cache frames.
  frame <pid> <index>     Select a frame and show its details.
  up <pid> [--count N]    Move to an older frame (default 1).
  down <pid> [--count N]  Move to a newer frame (default 1).

Stack frames display PC, SP/FP (when available), symbol, and source location.
Requires the executive's stack feature to be enabled.
