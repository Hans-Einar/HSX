stack
=====

Display the reconstructed call stack for a task.

Usage::

    stack <pid> [frames]

Arguments:
- ``pid``: Target task id.
- ``frames`` (optional): Maximum number of frames to walk (default: executive setting).

Shows per-frame PC/SP/FP along with symbol and source information
when .sym data is available. Frames are truncated if the executive
detects an invalid chain or reaches the frame limit.
